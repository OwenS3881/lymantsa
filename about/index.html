About

What is TSA?

TSA stands for Technology Student Association and is a club at Lyman High School. In TSA, we compete in a wide variety of STEM-related competitions each year. These competitions can range from software developnment, to mechanical engineering, to fashion design, and many others.

Competitions

Every year, we compete in two different competitions. The statewide conference is held at the end of February and is required for all members of TSA to attend. Before the state conference, we spend time preparing for our different events at the conference. The national conference is held at the end of June and is optional for members to attend. At the national conference, students who placed high enough at the state level can compete against students from all over the country. Learn more about all of the competitions here.
