Competition Selection

Every year in TSA, we compete in a bunch of different competitions at the state conference in February. Members can select up to 6 events to compete in. Since all events have a maximum amount of members that can participate, we ask that you also choose up to 3 alternate events to compete in if too many of your first choices have been taken already. An overview of each event can be found on the national TSA website here.

Competition selection is currently open and will be open until our third meeting (9/19).
